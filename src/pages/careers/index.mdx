# Careers

While most of the positions in Innovative Data Technologies (IDT) Lab at OSU are filled, 
the lab will be looking for exceptional students, who are interested in the areas of 
Data and metadata management, Storage and I/O, File systems, and Energy efficiency in HPC, 
Cloud, and Edge computing. If you are an outstanding student and interested in performing 
cutting-edge R&D in data management, please send your CV and transcript to Prof. Byna by email (byna.1@osu.edu).
